My Car Photography

"I've always had a passion for cars. To me, they can be an extension of the person that drives them. Sometimes they represent our persona, and other times they can be the opposite. No matter what enthusiasts will always find a way to be around them. Here are a few photos that I captured over the years being around cars."

-Larry L.

Here is a photo of a friend's Mazda RX7 in front of the Fox Theater. Also right behind his car is my RX7 that I owned at the time of this photo.
This photo was taken at Worldwide Technology Raceway. This was an event called "Midnight Madness", and here you will see cars lining up for the drag strip.
This is a photo of someone drifting a Nissan 240sx at the same Midnight Madness event. This is a popular car for drifting.
Here's a photo of a Nissan Silvia S13 and a Skyline GTR32. The Silvia S13 is the Japanese version of the 240sx, and was not released in US. The Skyline was also not originally available in the US. Enthusiasts import these after 25 years from the release date, which makes them exempt of any import laws.
This is another 240sx drifting at WWT Raceway. This was an amateur race where drivers compete for a spot at Formula D professional drifting.
Here is a Nissan S15 Silvia, which is a newer version of the S13/240sx chassis. This car also was not released in the US, so it is quite rare to see one since it hasn't reached the 25 year mark.
I took this photo of my car at Ikea. I enjoy shooting in parking lots; the painted lines can really bring attention to your subject.
Here's a photo of my friend's 240sx. This was at the racetrack during a drift event. Water puddles can bring a unique element to the picture.
Although Im not 100% sure of the model of this car, Im going to say its likely an 83 Toyota Corolla.
Here are a few Toyota Celica Supras. This was one of the earlier models of what would eventually become one of Japan's most iconic sports cars.
This is the rear end of a R32 Skyline Gtr.
And last, we have a Nissan 370z taken in the sunset.
